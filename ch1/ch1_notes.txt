** Area 1 **
grep obviously super helpful
	-B1 C1 A1 etc gives the lines before/both/after the grepped line
	-i is case insensitive
	-o is only matching parts of the lines
	 * is wildcard operator
example: cat file.txt | grep -i samuel | grep pap
- this above searches file.txt for case insensitive occurrences of samuel and then pieps those into grep pap which looks for occurrences of 'pap' in those results

pipe | takes output from the right and inputs it on the same command line
pagers
less command is like cat but it paginates
	- the slash command can search through less results
